About My MCU

My MCU is a site that once you create an account will be able to keep a running list of all your favorite Marvel Cinematic Universe(MCU) Movies!


The 'View Movie' page allows you to look through the current released movies. The list is in the correct timeline/watch order.


In the future there wll be a list of all the TV shows within the MCU as well, just like the list of movies already on the site. There will also be a future Marvel projects portion of the site to allow you to visit what's to come.
